the-resources on GitHub

by the LL, 2020 Spring

When we were moved off campus, the LL needed a way to collaborate on, create, and publish resources remotely. That became the-resouces on GitHub; the creation of which required every LL staff member and fellow to get to know GitHub & Markdown. Whie this was largely a "state-of-emergency" endeavor, the output of workflows & habits it will create will help out the LL's projects even after coming back to the Studio!
